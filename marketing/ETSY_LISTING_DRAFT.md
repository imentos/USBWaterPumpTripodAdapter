## Main Listing Description

This is a 3D printed adapter/base that lets a common USB rechargeable water pump mount to standard 1/4"-20 camera accessories.

Many rechargeable water pumps are designed to sit directly on top of a 5-gallon water jug. That works when the jug is directly under the pump, but it can be awkward for car camping, vanlife, RV, or portable hand-washing setups where the water container sits on the floor, inside a cabinet, or away from the basin.

This adapter gives the pump a standard camera-mount interface, so it can be used with common mounting accessories such as:

- camera clamps
- small tripods
- magic arms
- articulating arms
- shelf clamps
- RV or camping mounts

The goal is a removable setup without drilling into your cabinet, shelf, or vehicle interior.

## FAQ

### Does this include the pump?

No. This listing is for the adapter/base only. The USB water pump, hose, clamp, tripod, magic arm, water jug, and basin are not included.

### What pump does it fit?

It is designed for common USB rechargeable jug-top pumps made for approximately 2.16 in / 55 mm 5-gallon bottle openings. Please check your pump before ordering.

### Why use this instead of putting the pump on a counter?

These pumps are designed to sit on a water jug neck, not on a flat counter or shelf. This adapter gives the pump a stable removable mount when the water jug is on the floor, inside a cabinet, or away from the basin.

### Can I use it with a clamp instead of a tripod?

Yes, as long as the clamp uses a standard 1/4"-20 camera screw. Many camera clamps, magic arms, tripods, and small mounts use this standard.

### Is it waterproof?

It is intended for water-adjacent camping use, but it is not a sealed plumbing part. Avoid submerging the adapter or using it as a pressure fitting.
