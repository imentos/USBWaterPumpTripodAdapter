## First Product

**USB Water Pump Tripod Mount Adapter**

A 3D printed adapter/base that lets common jug-top USB rechargeable water pumps mount to standard 1/4"-20 camera accessories.

## Core Customer Problem

Common USB rechargeable water pumps are designed to sit directly on top of a 5-gallon jug.

That works when the jug is directly below the pump, but it becomes awkward when the water container is:

- on the floor
- inside a cabinet
- in a cargo area
- away from the basin
- used in a car camping / vanlife / RV setup

The pump needs a stable mount without drilling into shelves, cabinets, or vehicle interiors.

## Core Product Promise

Turn a common jug-top USB water pump into a camera-mount compatible pump.

USB water pump -> adapter/base -> standard 1/4"-20 camera mount -> clamp / tripod / magic arm / shelf mount

## Main Selling Points

- No drilling required
- Uses standard 1/4"-20 camera mount ecosystem
- Works with clamps, tripods, magic arms, and other camera accessories
- Useful for car camping, vanlife, RVs, and portable hand-washing setups
- Made to order
- Compact and simple

## Handling Negative Feedback

Some people already have sink-style pumps or simpler water jug setups. They are not the target customer.

Good reply:

That makes sense. If someone already has the faucet-style pump made for a sink setup, that’s definitely the cleaner solution.

I’m mostly testing this for people who already have the jug-top pump and want a removable way to mount it without drilling or changing their setup.

Appreciate the perspective -- it helps me understand where this does and doesn’t make sense.

- People use the same pump for car camping / van camping
