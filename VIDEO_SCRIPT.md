# Prototype Validation Video Script

Product: tripod adapter for rechargeable USB water pumps.
Audience: car camping, vanlife, RV, portable handwashing, DIY no-plumbing water setups.

## Goal

The first video is not a polished ad. It is a demand validation video. The goal is to get strangers to comment:

- I need this
- Where can I buy it?
- Would this work with my setup?
- I use these pumps for camping/RV/handwashing

## Core Insight

These rechargeable water pumps are normally made to sit on top of a 5-gallon water jug. But many people repurpose them for camping, vanlife, RVs, or DIY handwashing setups. Once the water jug is moved somewhere else, there is no good place to mount the pump.

This adapter lets the pump mount to a normal camera tripod or clamp, using the existing camera-mount ecosystem instead of custom hardware.

## Recommended Voiceover

```text

But a lot of people use them for camping, vanlife, or DIY handwashing setups.

The problem is, once you move the water jug somewhere else, there’s no good place to mount the pump.
```

## Filming Notes

- Vertical video.
- 20-30 seconds total.
- You do not need to show your face.
- Film hands, product, water jug, tripod, and water flowing.
- Record voiceover afterward in CapCut or similar.
- Add captions. Many people watch muted.
- Do not make it too polished. A real prototype demo feels more credible for validation.

## Validation Signals

Good signs:

- People ask where to buy it.
- People ask if it works with their pump.
- People describe their own camping/RV setup.
- People suggest mounting locations like cabinet edge, tripod, magic arm, table clamp, or RV wall.
- People ask for dimensions.

Weak signs:

- Only likes, no comments.
- People say cool but no one asks for one.
- People do not understand what problem it solves.

## Next Step After Posting

If 3 or more strangers ask where to buy it, create an Etsy prototype listing.

If 10 or more people comment with real use cases, make a small first batch.

If people are confused, reshoot the video with a clearer before/after setup.
